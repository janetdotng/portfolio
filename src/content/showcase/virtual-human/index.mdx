<p>Redefining digital presence — conceptualised and directed an in-house project creating four AI-generated Virtual Humans, designed to demonstrate a future where brands are no longer limited by physical creators. Through dynamic social media interactions, we showcased how AI face-swapping unlocks endless flexibility in storytelling. The project not only expanded the agency’s creative frontier, but also successfully inspired clients to adopt Virtual Humans for their own product campaigns.</p>
